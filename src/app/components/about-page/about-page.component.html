We Are There for You Along the Way

We are committed to reaching out to you and customizing the experience for your business’ needs. We take into consideration your preferences and always provide the most relevant news and updates to keep you informed, as well as suggest tenders on your dashboard that are similar to what you’ve already expressed interest in. Our dedicated, expert sales team is always looking to find the best solution for your needs, from helping you choose the most optimal subscriber plan to creating a custom plan all together. Assistance is always available along the way.

Our Vision

In the next few years, our vision is to operate as a group of companies headquartered in San Francisco and become USA incorporated in the name of Tenders2bid International Inc. The corporation would operate in 15 countries with offices for regional data collection and sales. With our ever-evolving AI ML software, we aim to remain at the forefront of allowing businesses and SMEs to concentrate on their growth while we make tendering a breeze. Now with offices in Australia, Canada, India, the United Kingdom, and the USA, Tenders2Bid is quickly becoming a true global marketplace for tenders and bids.
